### Medical Device Risks
- Windows 7 devices: Cannot be patched, on clinical network
- No network segmentation between medical devices and corporate
- Vendor access: 6 vendors have remote access to devices
- Patch management: Vendor-dependent, often years behind
- No medical device inventory management system

## Network Infrastructure

### Clinical Network (Sensitive)
- All 14 locations on single flat network
- No segmentation between clinical and corporate
- Medical devices on same VLAN as workstations
- Guest WiFi not isolated from clinical network
- HIPAA gap: Network segmentation required

### Connectivity
- MPLS to all locations (AT&T)
- Internet backup at main clinic only
- Remote clinics: Single circuit, no failover
- VPN: Cisco AnyConnect (MFA enabled)

## Storage

  - 85TB, growing 15TB/year

  - Backup: Local only, no off-site

1. No DLP solution (PHI leaving organization undetected)
2. Network segmentation inadequate
3. Medical device security program missing

5. Encryption not universal (80%)
6. Audit logging gaps for non-Epic systems
7. Incident response plan never tested
8. Third-party risk management informal

  - 50-100 attempts daily
