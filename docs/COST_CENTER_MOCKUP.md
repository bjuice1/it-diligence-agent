## Key Design Principles

### 1. **Lead with the Answer**
- Executive summary banner shows the three numbers that matter:
  - Total run-rate (what they spend annually)
  - One-time costs (what integration costs)
  - Synergy potential (what can be saved)

### 2. **Source Everything**
- Every number shows where it came from
- Data quality indicators show confidence level
- Enables "show your work" conversations

### 3. **Finance-Friendly Format**
- Cost tables match deal model structure
- Ranges (low/mid/high) for uncertainty
- Phase-based for cash flow modeling

### 4. **Actionable Insights**
- Not just data - commentary on what it means
- Prioritized concerns and opportunities
- Specific recommendations

### 5. **Deal-Ready Export**
- Excel export matches deal model format
- PDF for IC/Board presentations
- Copy-paste for financial models
