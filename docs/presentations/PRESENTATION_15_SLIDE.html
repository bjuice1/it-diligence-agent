Workflow

What You Actually Do

Step-by-Step Workflow

  1. Get VDR access ‚Äî Download key IT docs: application inventory, IT assessment, org chart, security review
  2. Upload to tool ‚Äî Drag and drop files. System auto-detects document type (application list? org chart? narrative?)
  3. Wait ~10 minutes ‚Äî AI processes documents, extracts facts, generates risks and work items
  4. Review Dashboard ‚Äî See summary: total risks, facts extracted, costs calculated
  5. Drill into risks ‚Äî Click any risk to see the evidence chain: Risk ‚Üí Facts ‚Üí Source Document ‚Üí Exact Quote
  6. Review Cost Center ‚Äî See run-rate costs, one-time integration costs, synergy opportunities
  7. Export for deal model ‚Äî Excel export for cost model, PDF for IC memo

Total time: 10 min upload + 10 min processing + 2-4 hrs review = Half a day vs. 2 weeks

What We Flag

Gaps

Information that should exist but doesn't. "No DR plan mentioned."

Unknowns

Items we couldn't identify. "Unrecognized application: XYZ"

Assumptions

Where we made judgment calls. "Assumed standard compensation."

Philosophy: Better to be honest about uncertainty than falsely confident. DRAFT label stays until human confirms.
